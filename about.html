About

Hi, I'm Krystine Monté!

I'm a young photographer with 8 years of experience based in Cardiff, South Wales. I started my career as 17 years old from a small village close to Cardiff. I had a lot of support from my family and friends to start my photography journey. I love my job, it gives me satisfaction and joy. It's a great feeling to keep someone's best memories of one of the important days in their life into a photograph.

I've photographed 150+ weddings and in this time I've really sharpened my skills to photograph the greatest and beautiful moments that happen throughout a wedding day. Weddings are about relationships, emotions and family connections, I do my best work to capture them for you.

In my spare time I really love riding a horse and being in nature with my family. I do have a lovely husband and two little girls. We spend our time together camping or sightseeing in cities. As a photographer I believe that we have to live our life with the loved ones as much as we can. I know that memories are very precious and important and that's the main reason I became a wedding photographer.
